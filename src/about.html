About

I am Kyle J. Maxwell, a California-born, Colorado-raised developer. Living in Colorado has has made me love the mountains and all the activities that go along with it, such as snowboarding and hiking. But I also enjoy experiencing the immense city, with the urban art that is all around. I am currently working and living in Philadelphia, PA. In my spare time I help design, photograph, and develop for Cold Denver, a clothing brand. Fashion is a very important aspect to me and I enjoy being a part of the culture in anyway.
